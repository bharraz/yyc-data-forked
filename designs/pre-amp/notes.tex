\section{Other tweaks}
\begin{enumerate}
\item $R_8$ and $C_{14}$ can be used for additional filter.
\item If a higher cutoff frequency is needed, reduce or remove $C_2$ to increase the
  input cutoff frequency.
\end{enumerate}
