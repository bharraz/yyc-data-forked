First we can bound the probability of the system being outside the perfectly correlated Hilbert space. If the probability of ion-photon pair $i=1,2$ being outside the correlated Hilbert space is $\varepsilon^i_C$, the probability of the ion-ion state being outside the correlated Hilbert space is,
\eqar{
  \varepsilon'_C\geqslant&\varepsilon_{C1}+\varepsilon_{C2}
}
so we have $\varepsilon^i_C\leqslant\varepsilon'_C$. This is not the best bound we can get but should be good enough.
